Where the silver generation meets the cubs

WIZtrans is a novel platform that brings together the two key demographics in a society - the young and the pioneers through a unique engagement model. With Singapore ageing rapidly, geriatrics care has gained policy focus. Also, research points to the numerous benefits of inter-generational bonding.WIZtrans provides a safe and reliable virtual platform for the interaction by linking eldercare centers with daycare centers and primary schools.
